Dropdowns guide you to pages with low-resolution previews of full-day animations of mountain webcams and a visible satellite. Webcams can go black or freeze on one frame.

Instructions:

  1. After all of the images download (roughly 200k each) refresh your browser to synchronize play.
  2. Click on image to get higher detail animation. Each frame is date stamped.
  3. Dates are given in YearMonthDay, e.g., 240101 is January 1, 2024.
